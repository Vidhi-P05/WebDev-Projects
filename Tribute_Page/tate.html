ABOUT TATE

Tate McRae is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and dancer who has taken the music world by storm. Born on July 1, 2003, in Calgary, Alberta, Canada, she first gained recognition as a dancer, placing third on the reality competition show "So You Think You Can Dance: The Next Generation" in 2016.

Tate's music career skyrocketed with her viral hit "you broke me first," which has amassed billions of streams worldwide. Her unique blend of pop and R&B, coupled with her emotionally raw lyrics and exceptional vocal ability, has established her as one of the most promising artists of her generation.

Beyond her musical talents, Tate is also known for her exceptional choreography and dancing skills, often incorporating elaborate dance routines into her music videos and live performances.
